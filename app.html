Use SignIt

Allow Download • A prompt will appear asking if you want to allow the download. Tap “Allow". Open Settings • After downloading, go to your device’s Settings app. You’ll see a notification at the top saying “Profile Downloaded". Tap "Profile Downloaded" • In Settings, tap “Profile Downloaded” to view the downloaded profile. Review the Configuration Profile • The configuration profile details will display. Tap “Install” in the top-right corner. Enter Passcode • If prompted, enter your device passcode to confirm. Tap "Install" Again • A warning may appear regarding the configuration profile’s authenticity. Tap “Install” again to proceed. Complete Installation • Once installed, tap “Done” to finish.
